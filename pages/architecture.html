Architecture

How I think about systems.

Core principles I use when designing backend and AI.

Start from the domain, not the stack.

Understand the domain and flows first; choose tools after.

Clear boundaries between services and modules.

Make it obvious who calls whom and what crosses boundaries.

Observability built‑in, not bolted‑on.

Design logs, metrics and traces up front, not later.

AI as a component, not magic.

Treat LLMs as one component with orchestration, validation and fallbacks.

Incremental evolution for live systems.

Change production systems in small, safe steps, not big bangs.
